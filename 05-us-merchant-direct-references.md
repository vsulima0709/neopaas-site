# NeoPaaS — US Merchant-Direct References (narrowed)

10 US-HQ startups that sell **directly to multi-location merchants** — the same audience as NeoPaaS. All funded 2022–2026.

This list replaces the broader `04-us-references.md` for merchant-positioning study. That earlier list is still useful for infrastructure ideas — but most of those companies sell to *software vendors*, not to merchants like NeoPaaS does.

---

## The key finding before the list

**No clean US merchant-direct multi-acquirer card-payment reconciliation platform exists.** The closest plays (Yuno, Primer, ACI) are orchestration sold to enterprises and PSPs — not to merchants. The vertical-financial-OS thesis is proven by Exponent and Lula Commerce, but no one has executed the reconciliation-specific version for merchant operators.

**This is a positioning advantage for NeoPaaS.** Use it.

---

## Closest functional reference

### ⭐ 1. Lula Commerce — [lulacommerce.com](https://www.lulacommerce.com)
- **HQ:** Philadelphia, PA
- **What they do:** Digital commerce + ops OS for multi-location convenience/liquor/dollar/QSR chains. Centralizes ordering across Uber Eats, DoorDash, Grubhub, plus first-party ordering, AI voice, and back-office reconciliation.
- **Who they sell to:** Multi-branch retail operators — Jacksons Food Stores, Par-Mar, Circle K, Shell, Valero, Clipper Petroleum.
- **Funding:** $8M Series A, Oct 2025, led by SEMCAP AI (Rich Products Ventures, GO PA Fund, UP.Partners). Total >$16M. [source](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2025/10/28/3175337/0/en/Lula-Commerce-Raises-8-Million-Series-A-to-Power-the-Next-Generation-of-Digital-Retail.html)
- **Why this is THE reference for NeoPaaS:** Three copyable moves:
  1. **Three-bucket nav segmentation** — Lula Hub (customer-facing) / Operators (back-office AI) / Managed Services. NeoPaaS can mirror exactly: payment surface / reconciliation back-office / managed onboarding.
  2. **"Retail Types" mega-menu** by vertical (Convenience / Dollar / Liquor / Specialty / QSR) with one-line operator pain per vertical — direct template for NeoPaaS "Merchant Types."
  3. **Dedicated `/financial-auditing-reconciliation` page** with the EXACT pattern NeoPaaS needs: hero "Boost your bottom line with unified reporting" + 5 icon-bullets ("Aggregate payouts from all delivery partners in one clean view," "Break down sales/tax/adjustments/fees," "Flag inconsistencies or missing funds across platforms," "No more manual CSV uploads," "Reconcile across all your stores from one dashboard"). **Swap "delivery partners" → "acquirers/PSPs" and the structure ports 1:1.**

## Restaurants / F&B

### 2. Restaurant365 — [restaurant365.com](https://www.restaurant365.com)
- **HQ:** Irvine, CA
- **What they do:** Cloud restaurant management suite — accounting, AP automation, inventory, workforce, payroll — for multi-unit operators.
- **Who they sell to:** Restaurant operators direct, segmented into Small Business / Multi-Location Groups / Franchise Brands / Accounting Firms. Customers: Sbarro, Whataburger franchisees, Black Bear Diner, Blaze Pizza.
- **Funding:** $175M led by ICONIQ Growth (KKR, L Catterton), May 2024, $1B+ valuation. [source](https://techcrunch.com/2024/05/15/restaurant365-orders-in-175m-at-a-1b-valuation-to-supersize-its-food-service-software-stack/)
- **Steal:** **"Who We Serve" mega-menu split on TWO axes** (Business Type × Restaurant Style) so operators self-select in seconds. NeoPaaS can mirror with Merchant Size × Vertical. Three-metric proof bar on the multi-location page: *"50% less time on accounting / 5% lower food costs / $100K reduced labor costs"* — outcome metrics, not feature counts. Headline pattern *"All-in-One Software for Multi-Location Restaurants"* → *"All-in-One Card Operations Software for Multi-Branch Merchants."*

### 3. MarginEdge — [marginedge.com](https://www.marginedge.com)
- **HQ:** Arlington, VA
- **What they do:** Restaurant back-office — invoice processing, bill pay, cost management, inventory, recipes. Bridges POS to accounting.
- **Who they sell to:** Independent operators and multi-unit groups direct. 11,000+ clients: Cava, Honey Baked Ham, Clyde's, Sunday in Brooklyn.
- **Funding:** $45M Series C, Dec 2022, led by Ten Coves Capital (Fiserv, Derive Ventures). [source](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/marginedge-secures-45-million-in-series-c-funding-to-empower-restaurateurs-with-actionable-data-and-insights-301697201.html)
- **Steal:**
  1. **Transparent flat per-location pricing above the fold:** *"$350 per month, per location. Simple as that."* Steal verbatim as *"SAR X per branch per month. That's it."*
  2. `/for-multi-units` page with the exact taxonomy NeoPaaS needs: Reporting (compare locations side-by-side) → Bill Payments → Recipes → Transfers → Onboarding. Reframe as *"compare settlements side-by-side by branch, acquirer, terminal."*
  3. Sales phone pinned in top bar — operator-direct trust signal.

### 4. Owner.com — [owner.com](https://www.owner.com)
- **HQ:** Palo Alto, CA
- **What they do:** All-in-one digital platform — website, online ordering, branded mobile app, marketing automation, loyalty.
- **Who they sell to:** Independent owners and growing multi-location operators (Talkin Tacos $7M direct sales, Saffron Indian Kitchen 4+ locations).
- **Funding:** $120M Series C, May 2025, led by Meritech + Headline at $1B valuation. [source](https://www.restaurantbusinessonline.com/technology/tech-supplier-ownercom-raises-120m-giving-it-1b-valuation)
- **Steal:**
  1. **Outcome-anchored case-study wall above the fold** — 15+ tiles, each a single number + owner name + concept (*"$192,000 Sales growth in 30 days — Hengameh Stanfield, Mattenga's Pizzeria"*). Copy verbatim: a wall of *"Owner Name + Concept + Single Number"* tiles for "SAR reconciled" or "hours saved per location."
  2. **Nav segmented by job-to-be-done, not features**: *"Grow online discovery / Grow online sales / Run your restaurant."*
  3. **Free Restaurant Grader** at grader.owner.com — NeoPaaS should ship a *"Multi-Acquirer Fee Grader"* top-of-funnel hook.

### 5. Slang.ai — [slang.ai](https://slang.ai)
- **HQ:** New York, NY
- **What they do:** Voice AI phone-answering for restaurants — inbound calls, reservations, FAQs 24/7.
- **Who they sell to:** Multi-location restaurant groups: Texas de Brazil, Merchants Hospitality Group, Fireman Hospitality, Carmine's.
- **Funding:** $36M Series B, Feb 2026, led by US Venture Partners with Thayer and Claire Hughes Johnson. Total $68M. [source](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/slang-ai-raises-36m-series-b-to-scale-ai-for-guest-communications-across-every-restaurant-302695306.html)
- **Steal:**
  1. **Three benefit-stat triplets under hero** with icon + bold metric: *"Drive up to 50% more phone covers / Save 200+ hours every month / Setup in 30 mins, 20x ROI."* Steal exact pattern: *"Reconcile 100% of payouts / Save 40+ finance hours/mo per branch / Live in 14 days."*
  2. **Operator-role quote attribution** — quotes titled *"Director of Analytics and Payroll,"* *"Events & Reservations Manager,"* never generic *"CEO."* NeoPaaS should attribute quotes to *"Head of Finance, 14 branches."*

### ⭐ 6. Exponent — [exponent.fi](https://www.exponent.fi)
- **HQ:** New York, NY
- **What they do:** Financial OS for multi-location franchise operators — AI accounting + corporate charge card with embedded microfinance + expansion/acquisition lending.
- **Who they sell to:** Multi-unit franchise operators 5–50 units. Customers: HAZA Group (largest Wendy's franchisee), Capstone Restaurant Group, Chunara Group, Epic Group. 100+ operators / 10,000+ locations.
- **Funding:** $40M+ total, May 2026 ($7.5M Series A + $30M+ credit), led by Chailease and Andre Koo. [source](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/exponent-raises-40m-to-build-the-financial-operating-system-for-americas-franchise-operators-302772605.html)
- **Why this is the cleanest copyable positioning in the set:** *"Financial Operating System for America's Franchise Operators"* is the same archetype as *"Card-payment Operations OS for Saudi Multi-Branch Merchants."*
  1. **Headline pattern:** *"Financial OS for [specific operator persona]"*
  2. **Named-customer-by-tier social proof:** *"largest Wendy's franchisee"*
  3. **Dual-axis proof bar:** *"100+ operators / 10,000+ locations"*
  4. **Bundling modules under one operator-persona narrative** rather than selling modules separately.

## Hotels / Hospitality

### 7. TripleSeat — [tripleseat.com](https://tripleseat.com)
- **HQ:** Concord, MA
- **What they do:** Event/group sales and catering management for restaurants, hotels, venues.
- **Who they sell to:** Multi-location restaurant groups, hotel F&B teams, venue operators managing private events.
- **Funding:** Strategic growth investment from General Atlantic, April 2023, ~$500M valuation. [source](https://www.generalatlantic.com/media-article/premier-event-management-platform-tripleseat-announces-strategic-investment-from-general-atlantic/)
- **Steal:** Rare clean example of a vertical SaaS selling the SAME product to two distinct multi-location buyer profiles under one brand. TripleSeat keeps **separate landing/solution paths for "Restaurants" vs. "Hotels" vs. "Unique Venues"** rather than a generic "Businesses" page — copy directly for NeoPaaS Restaurants vs. Retail vs. Hospitality. Case-study library organized by venue type and chain size — same "prove we work at our buyer's scale" problem NeoPaaS has.

### 8. Stayntouch — [stayntouch.com](https://www.stayntouch.com)
- **HQ:** Bethesda, MD
- **What they do:** Cloud-native hotel PMS with native payments, booking engine, kiosk, channel manager, 1,400+ integrations.
- **Who they sell to:** Hoteliers, explicit "Who We Serve" segmentation: Brands & Chains / Independent & Boutique / Management & Ownership groups.
- **Funding:** $48M strategic growth equity, Dec 2023, led by Sixth Street Growth. [source](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/stayntouch-raises-48-million-in-strategic-growth-equity-investment-led-by-sixth-street-growth-302011738.html)
- **Steal:** Cleanest multi-property segmentation in the batch — three distinct pages (**Brands & Chains / Independent & Boutique / Management & Ownership Groups**), each with portfolio-specific copy: *"Maximize profits, streamline performance, and standardize processes across your portfolio."* Exact framing NeoPaaS needs for single-store vs. multi-branch vs. franchise-management buyers. Homepage leads with **giant "Powerful Multi-Property Management" product screenshot** showing the cross-property dashboard view — direct visual template for NeoPaaS's "one dashboard for every store" promise.

## Retail / Multi-Location Operations

### 9. Delightree — [delightree.com](https://www.delightree.com)
- **HQ:** San Francisco, CA
- **What they do:** Unified mobile-first operations platform for multi-unit brands — centralizes location launches, audits, training, day-to-day ops across every site in one dashboard.
- **Who they sell to:** Multi-unit franchise brands and PE firms investing in franchises (sells direct to operators).
- **Funding:** Series B, Dec 2025, $23.6M total raised; Accel, Alchemist Accelerator, Axilor Ventures. [source](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/delightree)
- **Steal:** *"One platform to launch, run, and improve every location"* hero is a near-perfect tonal match for *"one dashboard for every store."*
  1. **Verb-triplet hero** (*"launch, run, improve"* → *"reconcile, comply, monitor"*)
  2. **"Why Delightree" sub-nav segmented by buyer type** (Operators / PE / Multi-Brand) — maps directly to NeoPaaS's vertical persona pages
  3. **Location Launcher as a named, productized workflow** — NeoPaaS could brand *"Terminal Onboarding"* or *"ZATCA Switch-On"* the same way.

## Logistics / Fleet

### 10. Onfleet — [onfleet.com](https://onfleet.com)
- **HQ:** San Francisco, CA
- **What they do:** Last-mile delivery management — route optimization, dispatch, driver app, real-time tracking, proof of delivery — across operator's own drivers plus outsourced couriers.
- **Who they sell to:** Multi-location shippers and courier/logistics operators with their own driver fleets: Kroger, Total Wine, United Supermarkets, Pizza Hut, Starbucks, UrbanStems.
- **Funding:** $23M Series B, June 2022, led by Kayne Partners (Savant Growth). [source](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20220607005530/en/Onfleet-Raises-$23M-Series-B-Funding-to-Help-Businesses-Optimize-and-Scale-Last-Mile-Delivery-Operations)
- **Steal:**
  1. **"By Role / By Industry" solutions matrix** — single platform sliced into vertical landing pages (Pharmacy, Grocery, Auto Parts, Beverages, Cannabis, Restaurant, Furniture, Construction) — directly mirrors how NeoPaaS should segment Retail chains / F&B chains / Fuel & fleet / Pharmacy chains.
  2. **"End-to-End Visibility" framing for a hybrid network** — *"Track every delivery in real time, whether internal or outsourced… unified analytics across all fleets and delivery partners to compare performance"* — near drop-in template for NeoPaaS's "one dashboard for every store/vehicle/acquirer."
  3. **Hero stat block** (*"99% on-time / 45% fuel cost savings / 50% reduced failed deliveries"*) — model for reconciliation / uptime / chargeback metrics.
