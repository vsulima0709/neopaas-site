Questions operators actually ask.

Will this work with my current POS / cashier system?

Almost certainly. We're already integrated with FOODICS, OASIS, ERPNext, Odoo, and a long list of others. If yours isn't on the list, we'll add it during your week-one setup. No SDK to install on your side.

Will this work with my current bank / acquirer?

Yes. We route through Mada, NeoLeap, Geidea, NmoHub, Arab Bank, and others. You keep your existing acquirer relationships. We just orchestrate across them.

What if a terminal goes down?

You'll see it in the dashboard within 60 seconds — branch, terminal ID, last successful transaction, current connectivity status. Your operations team can dispatch before a single customer is impacted.

How long until we're live across every store?

Two weeks from signed contract. Week 1: we integrate. Week 2: we train your team and switch branches over one at a time. Day 14: every terminal is on NeoPaaS, reconciling, filing.

What does support look like?

24/7 technical, Arabic and English. Slack channel for your finance and ops teams. Dedicated account manager. 99.99% uptime SLA with credit-backed guarantees. Response time under 15 minutes for critical issues.

What if we want to leave?

90-day notice, no penalty. We hand you back your full transaction history, your settlement records, and your ZATCA filings in standard formats. You're never locked in by data.

Is this really better than what my bank gives me?

Your bank gives you transaction processing. We give you a platform layer on top — across every bank you use, with reconciliation, ZATCA filing, terminal monitoring, and a real dashboard. Your bank's portal stops at "your transactions on us." Ours covers your entire business.
