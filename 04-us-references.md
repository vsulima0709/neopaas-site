# NeoPaaS — US References (payment orchestration + merchant infra)

17 US-based startups in the same space as Payrails, Gr4vy, Fonoa, and Chift. All funded 2022–2026, verified live, picked for what makes their site/positioning **worth studying** — not for competitive comparison.

Grouped by theme. Each entry: what they do, funding, one specific stealable move.

---

## Payment orchestration / infrastructure

### 1. Finix — [finix.com](https://finix.com)
- **HQ:** San Francisco, CA
- **What:** End-to-end payment infrastructure + PayFac-as-a-Service for SaaS, marketplaces, merchants.
- **Funding:** $75M Series C, Oct 2024, led by Acrew Capital (Leap Global, Lightspeed). [source](https://finix.com/press/announcing-finix-series-c)
- **Steal:** Single-line hero + giant dashboard screenshot, immediately followed by a **4-stat credibility wall** (tens of billions processed · hundreds of API configs · 99.999% uptime · billions of API calls) — the exact post-hero block NeoPaaS needs. Also: a dedicated `/compare-alternatives` page that names Stripe/Adyen directly, and split pricing pages by audience (Direct Merchants / Platforms / Payouts) — clean playbook for NeoPaaS's dual merchant/partner segmentation.

### 2. Moov — [moov.io](https://moov.io)
- **HQ:** Cedar Falls, IA (Golden, CO office)
- **What:** Unified money-movement (accept, store, send, spend) across cards, ACH, RTP, wallets, card issuing — one integration.
- **Funding:** $45M Series B, 2023, led by Commerce Ventures (a16z, Bain Capital Ventures, Visa). [source](https://moov.io/blog/company/moov-announces-series-b-funding/)
- **Steal:** Best-in-class **"verbs as IA"** navigation — *Accept / Store / Send / Spend* is the spine of both nav and homepage. Mid-page comparison table pits Moov vs "Other providers" across 5 rows. Sub-nav also grouped by software industry (Construction, Loan servicing, Transport) — the by-vertical approach NeoPaaS needs for cashier/ERP partners.

### 3. Payabli — [payabli.com](https://www.payabli.com)
- **HQ:** Miami, FL
- **What:** Payments infrastructure that turns software companies into payment companies (PayFac capability without becoming a PayFac).
- **Funding:** $28M Series B, June 2025, led by Fika Ventures + QED Investors. [source](https://www.payabli.com/news/payabli-lands-28m-series-b-to-accelerate-payments-infrastructure-for-software-companies/)
- **Steal:** *"Make software companies payments companies"* — 8-word elevator pitch, sharpest in the category. Stays narrowly vertical (healthcare, field services, property management) instead of going horizontal — a model for NeoPaaS to commit to one ICP at a time.

### 4. Skyflow — [skyflow.com](https://www.skyflow.com)
- **HQ:** Palo Alto, CA
- **What:** Data privacy vault (PII, PCI, PHI) sitting between your stack and sensitive data. Skyflow-for-Payments product reduces PCI scope.
- **Funding:** $30M Series B extension, March 2024, led by Khosla Ventures. [source](https://www.skyflow.com/press/data-privacy-startup-skyflow-raises-30-million-in-funding-led-by-khosla-ventures)
- **Steal:** Hero is a stark **3-word headline** with no subhead — confident move worth studying. Solutions menu sliced two ways simultaneously: by use case AND by compliance regime (HIPAA / PCI / GDPR / CCPA, each with flag emoji) — exactly the multi-axis nav NeoPaaS needs for ZATCA + GCC tax overlap.

### 5. Tilled — [tilled.com](https://www.tilled.com)
- **HQ:** Boulder, CO
- **What:** PayFac-as-a-Service for software companies to embed and monetize merchant payments (US + Canada).
- **Funding:** $12.5M, October 2024, led by Canvas Ventures + UPC Capital Ventures. [source](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/tilled-raises-12-5m-expands-card-present-strategy-internationally-302289126.html)
- **Steal:** Commits a top-nav slot to **"Why PayFac-as-a-Service?"** — treats category education as a primary CTA. NeoPaaS should do the same for "payment orchestration" since it's still unfamiliar in KSA. Also publishes a public **Product Journal** (changelog-as-marketing) — credibility move worth copying.

### 6. Conduit — [conduitpay.com](https://conduitpay.com)
- **HQ:** Boston area (Delaware-incorporated)
- **What:** Stablecoin payment rails for cross-border B2B — one API for fiat ↔ stablecoin orchestration as a SWIFT alternative.
- **Funding:** $36M Series A, May 2025, co-led by Dragonfly + Altos Ventures (Sound Ventures, Circle Ventures, DCG). [source](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20250528156066/en/Conduit-Raises-$36-Million-Series-A-to-Scale-Use-of-Stablecoins-for-Cross-Border-Payments)
- **Steal:** Tagline *"Anydollar. Anytime. Anywhere."* — 3-beat construction worth studying as a NeoPaaS hero formula. Lead proof point is a single concrete metric: *"$10B+ annualized payment volume, 16x growth in 2024"* — no feature laundry.

### 7. Mesh — [meshpay.com](https://www.meshpay.com)
- **HQ:** San Francisco, CA
- **What:** Global crypto payments network connecting 300+ wallets/exchanges so merchants accept stablecoin/crypto from existing user accounts.
- **Funding:** $82M Series B, March 2025, led by Paradigm (Consensys, QuantumLight). [source](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/mesh-secures-82m-in-series-b-funding-to-build-first-global-crypto-payments-network-302398543.html)
- **Steal:** **"Network" framing instead of "platform"** — they're "the first global crypto payments network." Lean on partner logos (MetaMask, Shift4, Revolut, 400M users in 100+ countries) as the primary credibility move. NeoPaaS can borrow the network-effect frame for cashier + acquirer + ZATCA pipe story.

## E-invoicing / tax / compliance

### 8. Anrok — [anrok.com](https://www.anrok.com)
- **HQ:** San Francisco, CA
- **What:** Real-time sales tax + VAT + GST compliance across 100+ countries for SaaS and AI companies.
- **Funding:** $55M Series C, Oct 2025, led by Spark Capital (Sapphire, Khosla, Sequoia, Index). [source](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20251015978298/en/Anrok-Raises-Series-C-to-Bring-Funding-to-Over-$100-Million)
- **Steal:** Hero stacks **three real customer quotes inline next to the headline** (Notion, Jasper, Sourcegraph) — high-trust opening move. Footer lists VAT rates for 80+ countries as individual SEO pages including Saudi Arabia — the long-tail compliance-content moat NeoPaaS should build for ZATCA phases + GCC tax. Dedicated **Atlas (agentic AI)** product page slotted into main nav — model for AI-native repositioning.

### 9. Numeral — [numeralhq.com](https://www.numeralhq.com)
- **HQ:** San Francisco, CA
- **What:** AI-powered end-to-end sales tax automation for e-commerce and SaaS.
- **Funding:** $35M Series B, Sept 2025, led by Mayfield (Benchmark, Uncork, YC). [source](https://techcrunch.com/2025/09/18/numeral-raises-35m-to-automate-sales-tax-with-ai/)
- **Steal:** How a second-mover positions on the same problem ("complete end-to-end" + "AI") instead of feature parity. Ties proof tightly to volume processed (*$5B in transactions, 150k returns filed, 3.5x YoY growth*) — template for NeoPaaS milestone messaging at Series A.

### 10. Sphere — [getsphere.com](https://www.getsphere.com)
- **HQ:** San Francisco, CA
- **What:** AI-native sales tax / VAT / GST compliance with direct integrations into 100+ tax authorities worldwide.
- **Funding:** $21M Series A, Nov 2025, led by a16z (YC, Felicis). [source](https://techcrunch.com/2025/11/18/a16z-leads-21m-series-a-into-tax-compliance-platform-sphere/)
- **Steal:** Cleanest **"AI-native" positioning** in compliance — out of stealth late 2024, raised from a16z 12 months later, 7-person team. Customer list (Lovable, Replit, Deel, Eleven Labs) shows how to win the AI-cohort buyer. Single moat headline: *"direct integrations to 100+ tax authorities."*

### 11. TaxBit — [taxbit.com](https://www.taxbit.com)
- **HQ:** Draper, UT (HQ2 Seattle)
- **What:** Tax information reporting infrastructure for enterprises + government. Originally crypto, now broad enterprise.
- **Funding:** $130M Series B, April 2024, led by Haun Ventures, IVP, Insight Partners at $1.33B valuation. [source](https://www.taxbit.com/blogs/taxbit-raises-130m-series-b-at-1-33b-valuation-to-disrupt-legacy-tax-information-reporting)
- **Steal:** Enterprise / **regulator-grade reference point** — they position as serving the IRS itself, giving them a "trusted by regulators" moat NeoPaaS could replicate with ZATCA. Their pivot from crypto-only to broad tax reporting is a useful narrative arc for expansion beyond initial niche.

## Unified APIs / merchant data

### 12. Rutter — [rutter.com](https://www.rutter.com)
- **HQ:** New York, NY
- **What:** Unified API for B2B financial products — one data model across 75+ accounting, ERP, commerce, payments, ads platforms.
- **Funding:** $27M Series A, March 2022, led by a16z. [source](https://www.finextra.com/pressarticle/91891/e-commerce-api-rutter-raises-27-million)
- **Steal:** **Integration-as-marketing** — every integration is an individually clickable SEO page (NetSuite, Sage Intacct, QuickBooks, Magento). Customer logo wall (Mercury, Airwallex, PayPal, Payoneer, Parafin) scrolls in 3 independent rows. *"Rutter in Action"* pairs four customer stories each with one big quant (*"2.5 weeks to ship first integration", "$200k annual engineering savings"*) — clean template for NeoPaaS proof rows. 3-pillar product diagram (Auth / APIs / Monitoring) NeoPaaS could mirror for POS / ZATCA / acquirer story.

### 13. Apideck — [apideck.com](https://www.apideck.com)
- **HQ:** San Francisco, CA + Antwerp, Belgium (dual HQ — founder team is European)
- **What:** Unified APIs across accounting, HRIS, CRM, file storage.
- **Funding:** $7.5M Series A, Nov 2024, led by Airbridge Equity Partners + PMV. [source](https://www.apideck.com/blog/series-a-funding)
- **Steal:** Homepage SEO title: *"The Unified API for all the accounting integrations you need."* Their **landscape blog post** ("Embedded Finance Companies Landscape 2026") is a textbook example of category-defining content marketing — NeoPaaS could do "Saudi payment landscape" annually.

### 14. Heron Data — [herondata.io](https://www.herondata.io)
- **HQ:** New York, NY
- **What:** Document + financial-data automation for lenders and insurers. Extracts and normalizes bank statements, invoices, tax docs.
- **Funding:** $16M Series A, July 2025, led by Insight Partners (YC, BoxGroup, Flex Capital). [source](https://www.insightpartners.com/ideas/heron-raises-16m-series-a-to-bring-the-ai-revolution-from-silicon-valley-to-american-businesses/)
- **Steal:** **"Vertical AI on top of a fintech data layer"** reference. Pitch is single-customer-segment focused (insurance carriers + FDIC banks), processing 350k docs/week. Model for NeoPaaS to lead with one industry persona and one quantified workflow instead of generic "any business."

### 15. Atomic — [atomic.financial](https://atomic.financial)
- **HQ:** Salt Lake City, UT
- **What:** Embedded financial connectivity — direct deposit switching, payment-method updating, bill/subscription management.
- **Funding:** $10M Series B, April 2025, from Capital One Ventures, Citi Ventures, FNB Capital. [source](https://finovate.com/atomic-raises-10-million-to-boost-innovation-and-expansion/)
- **Steal:** **"Framework for connected banking"** positioning — defensible alternative to "platform" / "infrastructure" / "API" word choices. Sell to 195+ FIs including 9 of top 10 banks — NeoPaaS could test similar word choices for its own category framing.

## Multi-branch merchant tools

### 16. Owner.com — [owner.com](https://www.owner.com)
- **HQ:** Palo Alto, CA (remote-first)
- **What:** All-in-one tech stack for independent restaurants — website, online ordering, marketing, loyalty, direct delivery.
- **Funding:** $120M Series C, May 2025, co-led by Meritech Capital + Headline at $1B valuation (Sweetgreen + Cava CEOs as strategics). [source](https://www.restaurantbusinessonline.com/technology/tech-supplier-ownercom-raises-120m-giving-it-1b-valuation)
- **Steal:** **owner.com/c is their Series C memo published as a webpage** — the cleanest example of "memo as marketing artifact" in the genre. High-leverage move for NeoPaaS when announcing its next round. The entire site is anchored on *"help restaurants compete with the Goliaths"* — one enemy, one mission, repeated everywhere.

### 17. Slang.ai — [slang.ai](https://www.slang.ai)
- **HQ:** New York, NY
- **What:** Voice AI ("Superhost") for restaurants, hotels, and venues — handles guest communications across phone channels at multi-location scale.
- **Funding:** $36M Series B, Feb 2026, led by US Venture Partners ($28M equity + $8M debt). [source](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/slang-ai-raises-36m-series-b-to-scale-ai-for-guest-communications-across-every-restaurant-302695306.html)
- **Steal:** **"Vertical AI for multi-branch hospitality"** reference. Leads on a single proprietary-data moat statement (*25M real guest interactions*). NeoPaaS could mirror directly: *"X million Saudi merchant transactions"* as the moat headline instead of generic tech claims.
