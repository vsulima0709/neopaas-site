Customer story · Logistics

How a Saudi courier network replaced cash-on-delivery and automated the entire claim cycle.

A logistics operator with 200+ active couriers needed to replace cash-on-delivery with card payments — at the door, on the road, with portable terminals. They also needed every disputed transaction filed back to the bank automatically. We built both into the NeoPaaS layer.

What we built for them

Cash-on-delivery, gone. Couriers now carry portable terminals connected to the NeoPaaS cloud. Payment requests are sent from their back-office dispatch system straight to the courier's terminal. Customer taps, payment completes, NeoPaaS records the transaction against the order.

Full claim cycle, automated. When a settlement doesn't match an order, NeoPaaS auto-maps the discrepancy, attaches the supporting evidence (transaction log, settlement file, timestamps), and files the dispute to the bank. Zero human in the loop.

One dashboard for ops. Operations team sees every active courier, every terminal status, every transaction in real time. Stuck terminal? They reset it remotely. Courier swap? Reassign the terminal in two clicks.
